Shrimp Fried Rice

Description

This is a quick and easy recipe for shrimp fried rice.

  • 3 cups water
  • 4 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 1 cup fresh bean sprouts
  • 1/2 cup chopped onion

  • 1/4 cup chopped green onion
